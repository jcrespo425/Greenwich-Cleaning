Pricing guide

What shapes the estimate

Estimates are usually based on the size of the property, the number of rooms or restrooms, the service frequency, the current condition, and any extras or travel-support needs.

Residential cleaning

Home size, room count, condition, and frequency usually shape the estimate first.

  • Bedrooms, baths, and approximate square footage
  • Weekly, biweekly, monthly, one-time, or move-related timing
  • Extras such as oven, fridge, windows, or linen changes

Office cleaning

Commercial quotes are usually based on cleanable square footage, restrooms, shared areas, and schedule.

  • Square footage, restrooms, and workstations
  • Business-hours or after-hours timing
  • Special requests such as floors, glass, or restocking

Short-term rental turnovers

Turnover quotes are usually based on property size, booking timing, linens, and guest-ready needs.

  • Bedrooms, baths, and turnover window
  • Linens, towels, laundry, and reset expectations
  • Restocking, staging, or same-day turnaround needs

Pet care and home sitting

Care estimates change when the request includes overnight coverage, multiple pets, medications, or more check-in tasks.

  • Pet count, care style, and travel dates
  • Daily visits versus overnight coverage
  • Home-check tasks such as mail, plants, lights, and packages
