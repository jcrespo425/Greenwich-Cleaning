Cleaning scope

The scope is spelled out so homeowners and hosts can compare services more easily.

Standard cleaning includes

  • Kitchen wipe-downs, visible appliance exteriors, counters, sink, and floors
  • Bathroom cleaning for sinks, toilets, mirrors, tubs or showers, and floors
  • Dusting and floor care in living spaces and bedrooms
  • Trash removal and a final reset of the most visible areas

Deep cleaning adds

  • More buildup attention in kitchens and bathrooms
  • Baseboards, edges, and overlooked detail zones
  • First-visit catch-up work before recurring service
  • Extra time for homes that have gone longer between cleanings

Turnovers and extras

  • Inside fridge or oven
  • Interior windows and detailed glass work
  • Linen changes or light laundry support
  • Guest-ready turnovers and tighter scheduling windows
